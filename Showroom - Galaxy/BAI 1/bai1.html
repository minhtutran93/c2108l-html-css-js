UNESCO

UNESCO (United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization) is a specialized agency of the United Nations established in 1945. Its purpose is to provide peace and security by promoting international collaboration.

Locations:

Headquarter of UNESCO is located in Paris, France.
